> Week 02 Object Selection


The Olfa x Acto Penknife is able to cut with a great range of motion by tilting to curve the cuts. It is able to stab into paper and has a removable cap for safety. It is held onto like a pen and it is lightweight to carry around.
The Olfa Penknife has a flat surface body that cuts paper in a straight motion. The blade can be withdrawn and extended. Actions include tilting, stabbing and cutting.

After observing and considering both objects, we decided to work with the Olfa Peniknife as it has greater interactions than the other penknife. We hope to explore it various uses and create an experience for the user when they interact with it.

Edit: We changed our Object in the later weeks as we found that that we needed a penknife with a handle that has poor electrical conductivity. As such, we are working with the Olfa x Acto Penknife.
